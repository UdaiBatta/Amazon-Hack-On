# RELAY AI — Demo Video Script
**Total runtime:** 4 minutes 30 seconds  
**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover. One continuous flow. No cuts mid-demo.  
**Jingle:** *"The item never touched a warehouse."*

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

Before you hit record:

- [ ] Set `RELAY_AI_MODE=mock` — zero network risk on stage
- [ ] Open browser in full screen, zoom to 125%
- [ ] Have two tabs ready: seller app (`/`) and ops dashboard (`/ops`)
- [ ] Have a third tab ready: buyer view (`/buyer`)
- [ ] Fraud toggle at the bottom of the scan screen — know exactly where it is
- [ ] Phone charged if you're showing mobile buyer view
- [ ] Kill all notifications on your machine

## Background Audio Direction

| Element | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Tone** | Calm, confident, cinematic. Not hype. Not startup energy. |
| **Music** | Minimal ambient underscore — single piano line or strings. No beat. Under 90 BPM. |
| **Source** | YouTube Audio Library — search "Hans Zimmer ambient minimal" or "cinematic underscore no copyright" |
| **Volume** | 15–20% of voice volume. Barely noticeable — but empty without it. |
| **Build** | Bring music in at Shot 3. Swell slightly at Shot 8 (the map). Peak softly at Shot 9 (moneyshot). Cut clean on final line. |
| **Silence** | After "Until now." — 1 full second. After fraud interrupt fires — 2 seconds. These are your most powerful moments. |
